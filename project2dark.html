CHICKEN KADHAI

Chicken Kadhai is a flavorful Indian dish made with chicken, bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes cooked in a rich, spicy tomato-based sauce. The key spices include coriander, cumin, and garam masala. It’s typically garnished with fresh cilantro and enjoyed with naan or rice, offering a vibrant and aromatic culinary experience.
